About The Cash Lotto

TheCashLotto is a lottery intermediary service that purchases lottery tickets on behalf of its customers.

TheCashLotto is not affiliated with any lottery commission program or operator in Ghana, either directly or indirectly. We do not sell lottery tickets, and thecashlotto does not receive any commissions from lottery organizations in exchange for its services, nor do we share in the prizes.

When someone stakes one of the NLA games on our website or mobile app, thecashlotto works as an agent, placing the bet on behalf of the client on NLA staking machine and handing over prizes/winnings in accordance. To put it another way, thecashlotto serves as a facilitator between the user and the NLA-licensed lottery operator(s) in Ghana.
